Who are we?

We are a community of mountain bikers who love to share the knowledge we've gathered over the years.
We're here to help you start your mountain bike trail building journey.



FAQ


1. Do you make money from this?

No, we don't make money from this. This is a non-profit organization. It was made for the community, not for money.


2. Can I contribute my own trails or builds?

In the future, we will add a blog where everyone can share their builds and ask for building tips or just chat.


3. Is TrailForge for beginners or advanced riders?

TrailForge is a website for people wanting to start their trail building journey, the features range from easy to extreme in size, complication of building process, and time.


4. Do you review gear or just use your own?

We don't review gear, but this is a community driven site, so if you have any suggestions, send them to "Info@TrailForge.com".


5. Are the trails you show public or private?

Everything we show on this website is built on private property with permission of the owner. So they are private.


6. How can I get started with trail building?

You can visit our free tutorial, just click "Explore Now" or "Trail Building" in the navigation.


7. Are you affiliated with any brands or companies?

No, we are not affiliated with anyone, so we can give you the real info with no restrictions. This is a non-profit organization, it was made for community, not money.
